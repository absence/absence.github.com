Teleodynamics - glossary

A form of dynamical organization exhibiting end-directedness and consequence-organized features that is constituted by the co-creation, complementary constraint, and reciprocal synergy of two or more strongly coupled morphodynamic processes

Teleodynamics (eg life, semiosis) - p 270

* Figure 9.1: Orthograde = recursive self-reconstruction and reproduction of systems of constraints

* a dynamical form of organization that promotes its own persistence and maintenance by modifying this dynamics to more effectively utilize supportive extrinsic conditions.

* not identifiable with any particular constituents, or even any constituent morphodynamic process.

* organized with respect to its own persistence

* not bound to specific material substrates or component dynamical process

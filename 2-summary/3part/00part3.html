Part 3 Summary ~ Chapters 11-17 ~ Exploring the Implications

Deacon summarizes Part 3 as follows:

"Having laid the groundwork for an expanded science capable of encompassing these basic absential relations, I explore a few of the implications for reformulating theories of work, information, evolution, self, sentience, and value. This is a vast territory, and in these final chapters I only intend to hint at the ways that this figure/background shift in perspective necessarily reformulates how these fundamental concepts need to be reconsidered."
